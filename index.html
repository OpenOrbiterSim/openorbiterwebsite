About

The long history of Orbiter

  • November 2000

    3.. 2.. 1.. - Liftoff!

    The initial version of Orbiter is released.

  • April 2002

    The Next Step

    Orbiter 2002 is released.

  • March 2003

    Orbiter 2003 launched

    After 4 versions of Orbiter 2002, it's retired and Orbiter 2003 is launched along with the infamous Dragonfly.

  • June 2005

    Switching to high-definition textures.

    After 2 years since the previous version, 2005 is launched. It included high-definition textures as a separate download.

  • May 2006

    The famous one I

    Orbiter 2006 is released. This version would become one of the most popular releases.

  • June 2010

    The famous one II

    With Orbiter 2010 released, users and developers quickly move from 2006 to 2010. Many of the add-ons released to date started with Orbiter 2010.

  • June 2016

    All the new features

    Orbiter 2016 released with a lot of new features and APIs. This was the final release of the closed-source version.

  • July 2021

    Time to open-up

    "In order to keep Orbiter alive and allow others to work on it, I have decided to publish the sources under an open source license" - Martin Schweiger

  • June 2022

    Open Orbiter Sim

    To keep some aspects separated from the core repo, Open Orbiter Sim is launched.
